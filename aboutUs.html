About Us

Welcome to Keshen, your number one source for recipes. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of experiences. We focus on quality, taste, and ingredients you can find with ease.

We at Team Clutch, had this idea of making a community out of people with a shared passion for food, hence the birth of Keshen. Keshen first started out as a simple project and is still under development and once finished, will be something of a marvel in the future.

We hope you enjoy our recipes as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
Sincerely, Clutch
